## Chapter 25: FODMAP Reintroduction Tracker

*This chapter contains the tracking template for Phase 2 (Reintroduction). Photocopy these pages or download printable versions from the link in the Back Matter.*

### How to Use This Tracker

The reintroduction phase is a scientific experiment. For accurate results:
1. Test ONE FODMAP group at a time.
2. Use the test food listed for each group.
3. Wait 3 days between tests (Day 1 = test day, Days 2-3 = observation and washout).
4. If no symptoms appear, that group is likely safe (at that portion).
5. If symptoms appear, mark as a trigger and skip to the next group after symptoms fully resolve.
6. Do not test during illness, stress, or other disruptions.

### FODMAP Group Test Schedule

| Test Order | FODMAP Group | Test Food | Portion | Test Day |
|------------|--------------|-----------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Lactose** | Lactose-free milk → switch to regular whole milk | ½ cup | Week 5, Day 1 |
| 2 | **Fructose** | Honey | 1 tbsp | Week 5, Day 4 |
| 3 | **Fructans (wheat)** | Regular wheat bread | 1 slice | Week 6, Day 1 |
| 4 | **Fructans (onion/garlic)** | Cooked onion | 1 tbsp | Week 6, Day 4 |
| 5 | **GOS** | Canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed | ¼ cup | Week 7, Day 1 |
| 6 | **Polyols (sorbitol)** | Avocado | ½ whole | Week 7, Day 4 |
| 7 | **Polyols (mannitol)** | Mushrooms, cooked | ½ cup | Week 8, Day 1 |

### The Goal of Reintroduction

By the end of Phase 2, you'll know:
- **Safe foods:** FODMAP groups your child tolerates normally — eat freely
- **Portion-limited foods:** FODMAP groups your child tolerates in small amounts — watch serving sizes
- **Trigger foods:** FODMAP groups your child does not tolerate — avoid or minimize

This knowledge is the entire point of the Low-FODMAP diet. Don't skip Phase 2.

### When to Share with Your Doctor

Bring the completed symptom journal to every appointment. Specifically flag:
- 3+ days of "😣" or "😖" in a week
- Any weight loss or loss of appetite
- Blood in stool (seek immediate attention)
- New symptoms that weren't present before starting the diet
